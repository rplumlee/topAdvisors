About us

Our story

Founder and CEO Brandon Rigoni began his professional career as an independent insurance agent. He has experienced firsthand the flaws in the lead and client acquisition system. His past experiences have led Brandon to launch TopAdvisors.

TopAdvisors was born to take the legwork and uncertainty out for consumers searching for financial services. In addition to helping consumers, TopAdvisors seeks to provide high-quality leads to financial service professionals at no upfront cost.

The Executive Team

Brandon Rigoni

CEO

Brandon Rigoni was born in Missoula, MT and has been a Portland native for over 25 years. He attended Concordia University - Irvine where he earned his degree in business management and was a member of the men’s soccer team. He started his career working for his father’s insurance agency and soon after started his own independent agency. While growing his business Brandon recognized the inefficiencies for consumers and financial professionals to connect, leading to the birth of TopAdvisors.

Reid Plumlee

CTO

Reid Plumlee is a Portland native who attended Gonzaga University where he earned degrees in accounting and finance. After spending a few years as an accountant for small businesses and startups, he turned his sights towards web development, a long-time hobby.

Jayson Robidoux

Director of Account Management

Jayson Robidoux worked with over 160 private investors and families as an Investment Counselor at Fisher Investments, owned his own successful recruiting firm for over three years, and is now the Director of Account Management for TopAdvisors. Jayson is originally from Maine, has lived in the Pacific Northwest for almost ten years, and enjoys his spare time finding outdoor adventures with his wife and two young children.
