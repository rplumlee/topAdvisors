Tired of paying up front for bad leads? We are here to help you build your business through pre-qualified online referrals. By asking consumers what they need, we send you leads that are actively looking for your services, in your area. Our algorithm is unbiased - the only way to improve your ranking is to perform.


What is TopAdvisors?

TopAdvisors is an online marketplace where consumers are pre-qualified and then matched with the 10 best local financial professionals who are verified and top performing in the industries of insurance, investments, and loans.


What does it cost?

Our service is 100% free to the consumer.

We do not charge the financial professional per-lead or a monthly fee. Revenue is generated through a 25% split of fee or commission, for the life of the client, when leads generated through our platform turn into clients.


What does getting verified entail?

We pre-screen all of our financial representatives before we send them any business. This screening has several steps

  • Criminal Background Check
  • License Verification
  • Designation Verification
  • Verification of performance statistics

Can I pay to boost my ranking?

Absolutely not. It is very important to us to maintain objectivity when it comes to our broker selection. You can improve your ranking by demonstrating that you are doing great business for the clients we send your way.


How many leads should I expect?

Our priority is bringing you high quality referrals rather than a high quantity. You shouldn't expect as many as most lead services, but you should expect them to all be pre-qualified consumers actively looking for what you have to offer.
